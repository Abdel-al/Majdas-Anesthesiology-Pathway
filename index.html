University Plans Comparison

Plan B: ASU + Barrett Honors College

Major: Biological Sciences (Biomedical Sciences) or Neuroscience

Strategy: Apply as a Transfer Student with AS Degree.

  • Transfer: Seamless transfer of all 79 credits via AGEC-S. Start as Junior.
  • Barrett Honors: Apply for "Upper Division" transfer. Gives access to Mayo Clinic research.
  • Pros: Close to home, massive scholarship potential, new Medical School opening.

Plan C: U of A + Franke Honors College

Major: Physiology or Medicine (Pre-Health Track)

  • HEAP Program: Honors Early Assurance Program. Apply for early med school acceptance.
  • Transfer: Dedicated Maricopa transfer pathway.
  • Pros: Established College of Medicine on campus, strong research focus.

Plan A: Harvard University

Major: Neuroscience or Molecular Biology

Strategy: Apply as First-Year Applicant (Dual enrollment = Rigor).

  • Admissions: Use your 4.0 GPA + Organic Chem to show you can handle Ivy League work.
  • Credit: Likely will NOT transfer science credits. Be prepared to retake them.
  • Financial: 100% Need-Based Aid (No merit scholarships).
