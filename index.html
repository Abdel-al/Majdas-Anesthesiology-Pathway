Undergraduate University Plans

Harvard University

Admissions Strategy: First-Year Applicant.

  • Role of Dual Enrollment: Proof of extreme academic rigor.
  • Course Plan: Retake pre-med sciences at Harvard level (4 Years).
  • Financial: 100% Need-Based Aid.

Arizona State University + Barrett Honors

Transfer Strategy: Transfer 79 Credits. Junior Status (2 Years).

  • Major: Biological Sciences (Biomedical) OR Neuroscience.
  • Key Benefit: Mayo Clinic Research Partnership.
  • Action: Apply for "Upper Division Transfer" to Barrett.

University of Arizona + Franke Honors

Transfer Strategy: AS Degree Transfer Pathway (2 Years).

  • Major: Physiology or Medicine.
  • Key Benefit: HEAP (Honors Early Assurance Program) for Med School.
  • Action: Apply to Franke Honors College.
